Since 1924

A Century of
Unrivaled Precision

Founded in the heart of Naples, AURELIA was born from a singular obsession: the pursuit of the perfect drape. For nearly a century, we have served the world's most discerning individuals, crafting garments that are as much architectural feats as they are items of clothing.

Every stitch is a promise. Every fiber is a legacy. We do not follow trends; we define the timeless silhouette of power and poise.

The Pillars of AURELIA

Our Guiding Principles

Absolute Rarity

We source exclusively the top 0.1% of global fiber production. From hand-combed Mongolian Vicuña to the rarest Sea Island Cotton.

Artisanal Mastery

Our master tailors undergo a twenty-year apprenticeship. Every garment is the culmination of hundreds of hours of manual labor.

Generational Integrity

We build for the centuries. An AURELIA piece is not a purchase; it is a permanent addition to your family's history.
